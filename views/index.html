About Us

fAmTech is a modernize tech-enabled large-scale farm that tends to eliminate hunger and livestock scarcity by integrating a mobile app to enable order placements at conveniences; at the door steps of our customers, we deliver orders. No inconveniences, no energy and time wastage! We are reliable!! We are the best livestock-dealers!!! .

Our Mission

To establish a modernized tech-enabled world-class large scale livestock farm with subsidised local and foreign breeds of livestocks availiable throughout the country, year in year out.

Our Vision

To eliminate or reduce to the bearest minimum hunger (caused by scarcity and unaffordability of livestock produce) and poverty by providing readily availiable and affordable livestocks and produce in large numbers for both SMEs and consumers.

fAmTech embarked on its journey in 2024. Fuelled
by the desire to cultivate a vibrant ecosystem, the tech's inception was rooted in
the belief that digital farms could not only transform lives but also empower communities.
